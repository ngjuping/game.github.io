Quick Guide

Anytime in game, press ESC to pause the game.

Move with ← → ↑ ↓ on your keyboard

You may blink by pressing 'Space' whenever the cursor in front is present.

You can move the platforms, even the one you are standing on!

Bullets can burn ordinary platforms, but not steel and icy ones.

You have only TEN lives!

Find objectives! There are TWO objectives in this game.

Be careful of the hidden LOWER boundary!
